Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

AutoClip ("the App") is an AI-powered video automation tool that publishes content to TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook via their official APIs using n8n workflows. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Platform Access Tokens: OAuth tokens from TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook required to publish content on your behalf.
  • Account Identifiers: User IDs and page IDs needed to target the correct accounts for publishing.
  • Content Data: AI-generated videos, captions, and metadata created and submitted through the App.
  • Usage Logs: Timestamps and automation activity processed through n8n workflows.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate with TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook APIs
  • Publish AI-generated video content on your behalf
  • Operate and improve the automation workflows
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party API Data

This App accesses data through official APIs from TikTok, Meta (Instagram and Facebook). We only request the minimum permissions required to upload and publish videos. We comply with each platform's API Terms of Service and data use policies.

4. Data Retention

We retain access tokens only as long as necessary to perform scheduled uploads. You can revoke our access at any time through each platform's app settings, which immediately invalidates our ability to post on your behalf.

5. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of all data associated with your account by contacting us at: ronron964@walla.com. We will process your request within 30 days. You can also revoke access directly from each platform's settings to stop all future data processing immediately.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

  • TikTok API – for video publishing
  • Meta Graph API – for Instagram and Facebook publishing
  • AI generation services – for creating video content
  • n8n – for workflow automation

8. Your Rights

  • Revoke platform access at any time via each platform's settings
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: ronron964@walla.com
